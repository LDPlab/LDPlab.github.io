Summer Internship

In collaboration with UW I-LABS, the LDP Lab is thrilled to announce this year's Summer Internship! Three to five undergraduate and/or graduate students will be selected to work directly with Dr. Naja Ferjan Ramírez, between June 15 and August 21, 2020. The work will be related to the following two projects:

  • Madrid: English Language Intervention in Infant Education Centers
  • Language Input and Language Outcomes: The Parent Coaching Study

This is a paid internship (rate of pay $16.39/hr). Full-time and half-time positions are available.

To apply, please send the following materials to Naja Ferjan Ramírez (naja@uw.edu, subject line should be SUMMER INTERNSHIP – YOUR NAME), by March 13th.

  1. Your CV
  2. Your transcript (unofficial copy is OK)
  3. A short (200 words max) description of which project you are interested in, why, and how this internship may enhance your learning, and/or your academic or career path. Please also indicate whether you're interested in a full-time or half-time position.
  4. A confidential letter of recommendation from somebody who has mentored you or taught you in the past. This should be sent directly to Dr. Ferjan Ramírez, with the subject line SUMMER INTERNSHIP – YOUR NAME, LETTER OF RECOMMENDATION.

Phone or Zoom interviews will be conducted in March/April and final decisions will be made shortly thereafter.

Students:

Dr. Ferjan Ramírez is always interested in mentoring curious, passionate, and motivated students! If you are interested in hearing about upcoming research opportunities in the lab, please email her directly at naja@uw.edu.
