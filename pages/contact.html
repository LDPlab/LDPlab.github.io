Where to Park

We have a reserved parking spot for our guests in the Central Parking Garage (C1)!
Free Parking!
No need to pay for parking when you arrive. Just come into the garage and park in our spot!
We Meet You!
A member from the lab will be at the parking space to help and show you to the lab!
Stroller Accessible!
The garage is equipped with an elevator, so both wheelchairs and strollers are accommodated.

Google Maps of the Central Parking Garage (C1)

How to find us:

Use Google Maps to locate the Central Parking Garage on the UW campus.

Once you get to the garage, please stay on the top floor (C1).

There will be a parking space designated for LDP, where a researcher will be waiting for you.

If you plan on arriving early or late, please give us a call or send us an email in advance.
