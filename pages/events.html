Winter Quarter LDP Lab Meetings

In Winter Quarter of 2021, the LDP Lab meetings will be held virtually on Zoom. We will meet every other Friday at 9:00 AM (Pacific Time), starting on January 15. The first meeting will be organizational, and the topics of all subsequent meetings will be announced after the first meeting. If you'd like to be added to the lab email list, please contact Dr. Ferjan Ramírez by email.
