Welcome to our lab!

Located in Smith Hall 002, the Language Development and Processing Lab investigates how children and adults understand, speak, and learn language. Read more about the mysteries of language growth here.

Learn about our research!

The LDP Lab studies how children learn language by using a variety of child-friendly methods, such as eye-tracking and language listening tasks. Read more about our child-focused research in our papers section.
