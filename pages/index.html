The Language Development and Processing Lab is new to UW Linguistics. We also have a lab space located in Smith Hall 002. You can find out about directions, parking, and Smith Hall here.

Learn about our research!

LDP lab studies how children learn to use language by using a variety of child-friendly methods, such as eye-tracking, and language listening tasks. Read more about our child-friendly research in our papers section.
